wvz_polygon = pandas.read_csv('Daten/WVZ_Eckpunkte.csv')

(4) Key findings

1. Crime displacement took place

  • This is true for the total of police-recorded crimes as well as violence and theft
  • However, the Eisenbahnstraße and surrounding area remain the biggest crime hotspots even after WPZ implementation

2. Displacement effect differs between types of crimes and offenses

  • Total of police-recorded crimes:
    • crime inside the WPZ decreased for the first 6 month after implementation
    • strong rebound effect after the first 6 month, crime inside WPZ increased sharply
    • crime outside the WPZ reached an equilibrium between hot and coldspots after 12 month
  • Violence:
    • very similar to development of total crimes regarding hot and coldspots inside the WPZ
    • violence hotspots outside the WPZ remain even two years after WPZ implementation
  • Grand and petty theft:
    • strong reduction of theft inside the WPZ
    • sharp increase of new hotspots outside the WPZ
    • pattern solidifies over time
